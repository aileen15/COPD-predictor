Preprocessing.py will merge all single year csv files to copd_perc_all.csv
and multi-year (2012-2015) to copd_merge_2012-2015.csv

Prediction.py will use copd_perc_all.csv to predict single year binary or multiclass classification.

Prediction.py will use copd_merge_2012-2015.csv to predict multi-year binary or multiclass classification.
